Featured case ASICS HQ

A WAY TO
HEALTHY INNOVATION

We took ASICS’ mission statement - ‘a sound mind in a sound body’ - and gave it physical form by developing a new regional HQ that proactively stimulates a healthy and sustainable life and workstyle. For the design of Japanese brand ASICS’ regional headquarters, we drew on the architectural tradition of the palazzo, creating a central atrium space that is symbolic of

company values as well as facilitating social interaction. At the heart of the atrium we placed a three-dimensional showcase that displays the ASICS’ brand and functions as a podium, meeting space and design center. We replace the functions of a conventional office lobby with the atrium’s informal meeting space and a public and prominent exhibition space.
